About Tali

The Tali Sports Center is an indoor facility encompassing a wide range of programs and activities. Best known for volleyball and indoor soccer, TSC offers a variety of ways to get involved, including: leagues, camps, clinics, lessons, tournaments, parties and more.

Tali Sports Center is conveniently located just 3 miles southest of downtown Denver, Colorado.
